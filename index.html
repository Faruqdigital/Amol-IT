Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Amol IT?

    Amol IT is an internet-based e-learning platform. The platform actively promotes these courses within freelance marketplaces, making them accessible to a broad audience seeking to acquire new skills or knowledge.

  • How do I get started?

    To get started, simply register for a free account, browse our Courses page, and choose the course that suits your learning goals.

  • What support is available to me while taking a course on Amol IT?

    Amol IT offers 24/7 support to all our students. You can reach out to us via Zoom, and we provide lifetime support to all of our students.

  • How much do Amol IT courses cost?

    The pricing for our courses fluctuates depending on the content and level of complexity. We provide a range of both online and offline courses to meet diverse learning requirements.

  • Are all the courses recorded?

    Our all courses are held on live and after the class, we upload the full record on “Google Classroom” so that all the students can view and download it from Google Drive.

  • Do you have an official Facebook group so that I can get connected with your community?

    Yes, we have an official group that are "Amol IT" Official page from where we update many necessary things regarding our courses and student success stories.
