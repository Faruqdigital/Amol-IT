Beginning of Amol IT

Amol IT Institute is a leading computer training institute in Bangladesh. With over 10 years of experience, Amol IT Institute has a proven track record of providing quality education and training to its students. The institute offers a wide range of courses in computer science, information technology, and business management.

Amol IT Institute has a highly experienced and qualified faculty of trainers. The trainers are experts in their respective fields and have a passion for teaching. They are committed to providing their students with the best possible learning experience.

Amol IT Institute has a state-of-the-art infrastructure. The institute has a well-equipped library, a computer lab, and a multimedia room. The institute also has a strong online presence. Students can access course materials and interact with their trainers online.

Amol IT Institute is committed to providing its students with the best possible career opportunities. The institute has a strong placement record. Amol IT Institute graduates are in high demand by employers in Bangladesh and abroad.
